Budgeting for Young Adults

Tools used:

Articulate Storyline, Figma, Adobe Photoshop

Overview

Working and arranging your income is not only a numbers game, but also a skill reordering your priorities. However, how your priorities and incomes change, it’s hard to keep track of hidden expenses.This E-learning course is designed for young adults.

Contex

While writing your expenses with a pen and paper method can give you insight, it would be best to have a solid theory and practice that can categorize them all. Remember, our education system doesn’t give us any knowledge on how to arrange our income. We can ditch the boring lectures, and jump right away in this interactive and insightful E-learning course.

Challenges:

I found out that people are reluctant to tackle with their budget planning and incomes. Whether it’s for a simple night out, planning a vacation with a family, monthly expenses, it’s all different contexts, right? What you need is a proven method of arranging your income that is resilient to any sudden changes in your expenses.

Solutions:

The idea is to structure an E-learning course for any target group that wants to feel safe when they are planning their budget. I introduce real life situations that we all can resonate with, examples, games, and giving in-depth overview to the 50/30/20 method.

Results:

The end result of this E-learning course is to teach people how to not only save money, but to efficiently plan their budget that will make them feel as secure as possible. Through practice of the 50/30/20 method, you will see how any expense (small or large) can be precisely categorized, rearranged, and effortlessly planned to make your hidden expenses more visible and your income will increase.
