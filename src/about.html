about me

It’s a pleasure to meet you!


There’s a story tied to my passion for e-learning development. I wanted to combine my interests in computer programming and how people learn. We are different individuals who experience and learn in different ways. I wanted to make courses that will transform those differences or different learning styles into unique features (benefits) for the e-learning platform, so my clients can have the best experience.


The project that set everything in motion was during college, where I graduated in Pedagogy (Education department).I never felt more intrigued and involved in something than during that project, or any other regarding e-learning development. The project was ranked with the highest grade and that was the cornerstone for my future career.


But I can also assure you that besides my work, I always offer positive energy, authenticity, and quality. The road to a successful project is important too, not just the project itself, just like in the learning process - every day you learn something new on the road to knowledge.
